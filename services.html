❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our coaching programs.

How are the online classes conducted?
▼

Online classes are conducted via Zoom or Google Meet with screen sharing, interactive digital whiteboard, and real-time question-answering. All sessions are recorded and shared with students for revision.

What is the batch size for each class?
▼

We maintain small batch sizes (max 5 students) or offer 1-on-1 private sessions to ensure personalized attention. Group batches are also available at discounted rates.

Is there a free demo class available?
▼

Yes! We offer a completely free 45-minute demo session for all new students. This helps you experience our teaching style before committing to any plan. Book your demo via the contact page.

Can I switch between plans?
▼

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle. Contact us at least 5 days before your renewal date to make changes.

Do you provide study materials and notes?
▼

Yes, comprehensive study materials, handwritten notes, practice papers, and assignments are provided digitally in PDF format. Physical materials are available for offline students.

What subjects are covered under Science training?
▼

Science training covers two major disciplines: Physics (Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Optics, Electricity) and Chemistry (Physical, Organic, Inorganic).
