Common Questions

How quickly will I get a response?
▼

We typically respond within 2–4 hours via WhatsApp and within 24 hours via email. For urgent queries, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us.

Can I get a free demo class first?
▼

Absolutely! We offer a free 45-minute demo class for all new students. Simply fill out the contact form or message us on WhatsApp to schedule your free session.

Do you offer group discounts?
▼

Yes! We offer special discounted rates for groups of 3+ students enrolling together. Contact us to discuss custom group plans and pricing.

What platforms are used for online classes?
▼

Online classes are conducted primarily via Zoom or Google Meet with an interactive digital whiteboard. All sessions are recorded and shared with students within 24 hours.
